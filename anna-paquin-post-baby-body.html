Names of Anna Paquin's twins revealed

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

    Actress Anna Paquin was the centre of attention at the premiere, when she stepped onto the red carpet flaunting her incredible post-baby body, and when the names of her nine-month old twin babies were revealed. Anna, and husband and fellow co-star Stephen Moyer, have worked hard to keep the twins out of the media eye. However it was Stephen, 43, who let slip the names of their babies at the premiere. Speaking to , the doting father said he was looking forward to spending Father's Day "hanging out with Charlie and Poppy."

    Anna, 30, piped in she had planned something special for Stephen, and the twins' first celebration with their dad. "I can't tell you because that would ruin the surprise," she told . "I don't know about it being a big surprise, but yes, there are plans." Anna gave birth to the fraternal twins last September, and less than a year later she is already showing off her incredible post-baby body. The blonde beauty stayed true to her on-screen vampire character, and looked as fierce as ever in a short black David Koma dress with leather detail that flaunted her toned, tanned legs.

    She completed the dark ensemble with black Yves Saint Laurent heels and immaculate nails painted black, and brightened up her look with a radiant Jacob & Co. necklace and pointed Melinda Maria earrings. The co-stars attended the premiere of the show's sixth season in Hollywood on Tuesday night, and looked as loved-up as ever. They reportedly started dating soon after they met on set when filming the pilot episode of the show. The twins are the couple's first children, but they seem to be managing parenthood well with Stephen's expertise. He is father to Billy and Lilac from previous relationships.
