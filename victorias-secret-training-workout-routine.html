Train like an Angel! See how Victoria's Secret models prepare for the show

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 1, 2024

    With less than four weeks to go until they strut their stuff at the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show, models Jasmine Tookes and Josephine Skriver have stepped their workouts up a notch.

    The duo have been giving fans a glimpse at their training regime on their Instagram channel, and it looks intense!

    View post on Instagram 

    Jasmine Tookes shared a glimpse at her workout routine

    TRX, battle ropes and heavy weights have all been incorporated into the models' routine for a high intensity workout that will get them ready to walk the runway in Paris.

    "It's that time of year again and that means taking our workouts to the next level! Here are some moves you can give a try at the gym," they captioned a video of Jasmine at the gym.

    The video has already been viewed close to 30,000 times with many fans expressing how shocked and impressed they were with Jasmine's gruelling routine.

    Jasmine and Josephine Skriver have their own fitness Instagram account

    "She's incredible," one commented, while another added: "This is next level."

    Victoria's Secret Angels Jasmine and Josephine are both fitness fanatics, and regularly share photos and videos of their workouts on their Instagram channel JoJa.

    "We both really love fitness and health, and we wanted to give people a deeper look into our everyday fitness routine," Jasmine told about their decision to create the account, adding that working out together helps to keep them motivated.

    The pair are set to walk in the Victoria's Secret show

    Another motivator for the friends is surely the pivotal roles they are set to play in this year's fashion show; Josephine will walk the runway in the dazzling Swarovski outfit, while Jasmine has been given the honour of wearing the Â£2.5million Fantasy Bra.

    The show is set to be recorded in Paris on 30 November, with guest performances from Lady Gaga, Bruno Mars and The Weeknd.
