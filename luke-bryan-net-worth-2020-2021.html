Early Life

Luke Bryan was born on 17th July 1976 in the home of a farming family in Leesburg, Georgia. His father’s name is Tommy Bryan, and his mother’s name is LeClaire. He also has an older brother who died in a car accident, while his older sister Kelly is an organiser.

He studied at the Georgia Southern University, Statesboro and graduated in business administration in 1999. He joined the Sigma Chi fraternity in the college days. After that, he moved to Nashville in 2007 for pursuing a music career.

Personal Life

Bryan is happily married to his Carolin Boyer since December 2006. Together, they have two sons whose name are Tatum Tate Christopher Bryan and Thomas Bo Boyer Bryan.

He is a very passionate hunter and co-owner of the Buck Commander that team partnered with outdoor channels and make TV shows. Apart from that, this talented person can be actively seen supporting a range of causes, such as human rights, AIDS & HIV, disaster relief, and health.

Age, Height, and Weight

Career

Soon after transferring to Nashville, Bryan started working for a publishing house. Capitol Nashville gives him a recording contract, and he soon accepts it. In the same year, he co-wrote the single “Good Directions” sung by the Billy Currington. This song tops the Hot Country Songs chart.

However, the real breakthrough came in his career when Bryan’s first song “All My Friends Say” released, which ranked 5th position on the list of Hot Country Songs. The debut album of Luke Bryan “I’ll Stay Me” in August 2007 that has eleven songs. Almost all of them were a huge hit among the people.

He launched his second album Doin’My Thing in October 2009. After that, he wrote two singles named “Someone Else Calling You Baby” and “Rai Is a Good Thing” that reaches to the first position on the country music charts. After that, he has been part of many albums, such as Tailgates & Tanlines, Kill the Lights, What Makes You Country and Crash My Party.

Awards & Achievements

Since his perception in the music industry, Luke Bryan has won many awards in his career. In 2010, he was the winner of two Academy of Country Music Awards in the categories “Top New Solo Vocalist” and “Top New Artist.” Without any doubt, 2012 was the best year of his life when he won American County Awards in nine categories. He also won CMT Performance of the Year award in 2017.
