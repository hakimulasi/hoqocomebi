Taron Johnson family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 5, 2024

Table of Content

An American football cornerback that plays for the Buffalo Bills in the National Football League is named Taron Johnson.

Taron Johnson represented the Weber State Wildcats in college football and was honored as the Big Sky Defensive Player of the Year.

At Sacramento, California’s Sheldon High School, Johnson participated in high school football.

Due to his remarkable talent, he was promoted to the school’s varsity team as a sophomore, one year earlier than the norm. He would later become the first NFL Draft selection in school history.

Taron Johnson wife

Taron Johnson’s girlfriend, Meg DiMarco, is a stunning American woman who enjoys fashion and beauty. She is a cornerback for the Buffalo Bills.

Meg DiMarco is a huge Taron Johnson supporter and typically shows up to American football games to show her support.

Taron Johnson children

Taron Johnson has no children.

Taron Johnson parents

On July 27, 1996, Baron and Trina welcomed Taron into the world in Sacramento, California, the United States. His mother is Trina, and she comes from a line of athletes.

While Trina herself was a cheerleader, her father was heavily active in football. Because to breast cancer, his mother passed away at the age of 38.

He is also quite close to his family, who are very supportive of Taron’s career.

Taron Johnson siblings

The family includes an elder sister named Treana in addition to Taron. Treana competed in track competitions.
