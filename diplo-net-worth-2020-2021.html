Early Life

Thomas Wesley Pentz, better known as “Diplo” is the son of Thomas and Barbara Jean Pentz. He was born in Mississippi’s Tupelo on 10 November 1978. He has got a home-grown rapping style.

He went to Temple University and has also attended the University of Central Florida.

Personal life

Diplo is the father of Lazer and Lockett with Kathryn Lockhart. In the past, he has also dated M.I.A. and Katy Perry. He is a fan of cricket and soccer. He is also a fan of the Los Angeles Clippers.

Singer M.I.A. has been credited with the fame and success of the Diplo. They have worked together on many occasions.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 10 November 1978, Diplo is 45 years old as of today’s date 21st July 2024. His height is 1.8 m tall, and his weight is 86 kg.

Career

Diplo started his career by working as a DJ. He has done performances in places across the world. In 2004, he released his first and only studio album, ‘Florida.’ He has also released one live album, five compilation albums, 6 EPs, four mixtapes, and 35 singles.

In 2008, Diplo became one of the founding members of Major Lazer. A year later, the group released the album ‘Guns Don’t Kill People…Lazers Do’. Their album ‘Free the Universe’ became available from April 2013.

Soon after that, Major Lazer released ‘Peace Is the Mission’ in 2015. They are all set to release ‘Music Is the Weapon’ on 16 March 2018. Major Lazer has also released 4 EPs, 23 singles, four remix albums, and five mixtapes.

They have released many hit singles like “Watch Out for This (Bumaye),” “Bubble Butt,” “Lean On,” “Powerful,” “Light It Up (Remix),” “Cold Water,” and “Know No Better.” From 2013 to 2016, he was a member of Jack U along with Skrillex. Together, they released the album ‘Skrillex and Diplo Present Jack U’ in 2017.

Jack U also released the singles “Where Are U Now” with Justin Bieber, “To U” with AlunaGeorge, and “Take U There” with Kiesza. Their only album made the duo win many awards, including two Grammy Awards.

Awards & Achievements

As a solo artist, Diplo is the winner of two Grammy Awards. He won both awards in 2016. With Jack Ü, he won the 2015 American Music Awards and the International Dance Music Awards of 2016. The duo also won the MTV Video Music Award and was nominated for the NRJ Music Award in 2016. As a member of Major Lazer, he has received several nominations and is the winner of the 2016 Billboard Music Awards.
