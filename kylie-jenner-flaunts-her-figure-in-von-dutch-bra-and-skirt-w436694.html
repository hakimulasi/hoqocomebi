Kylie Jenner Flaunts Her Figure in Von Dutch Bra and Skirt

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 9, 2024

Kylie Jenner is bringing Von Dutch back! She may have skipped the 2016 MTV VMAs, but the reality TV beauty was still determined to make a style statement with her latest Instagram snap.

Jenner showed off her curves on Sunday, August 28, in a skintight outfit by the brand, that was became famous for its trucker hats.

But there wasn’t a cap in sight for the 19-year-old, who squeezed her assets into an all-white sports bra and miniskirt covered in Von Dutch labels.

She captioned the Sunday selfie with a simple sun emoji and the word “day.”

It’s not the first time the makeup mogul has rocked Von Dutch. She wanted the world to know that the brand is still alive and well when she shared a photo of herself wearing one of its hats in April 2016.

Jenner is back in Los Angeles after a fun-filled trip to Las Vegas with her boyfriend, Tyga, and his son, King Cairo.

They snuggled up for a social media snap on the private jet that flew them to Sin City, where the rapper was performing at Drai’s nightclub over the weekend.

Jenner was dressed down for the photos, wearing a pair of gray sweats and Converse sneakers, and she also kept it casual when she met with friends for a David Copperfield magic show during her trip.

The teen ditched red carpet glamor for an oversize T-shirt and white pumps.
