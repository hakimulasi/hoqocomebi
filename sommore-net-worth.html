Sommore Full Overview and Wiki

Early Life and Education

Born on May 15, 1966, in Trenton, New Jersey, Sommore’s early life was as normal as it gets. She attended Morris Brown College, where she studied business administration. Little did she know that her future would take a turn towards the limelight.

Breaking Barriers in Comedy

As the first female host of BET’s ComicView, Sommore shattered glass ceilings. Her unique blend of sarcasm and candid discussions on money, sex, and equality resonated with audiences.

She didn’t just perform comedy; she lived it, bringing a fresh perspective to the male-dominated world of stand-up. Her participation in the Queens of Comedy tour further cemented her status as a comedy powerhouse.

Television and Film Appearances

Sommore’s versatility shines through her extensive filmography. From laugh-out-loud roles in Friday After Next and Soul Plane to her appearances on shows like Def Comedy Jam and The Oprah Winfrey Show, she’s done it all.

A Stand-Up Icon and Achievements
