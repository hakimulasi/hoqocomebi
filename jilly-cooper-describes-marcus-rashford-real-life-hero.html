Author Jilly Cooper describes Marcus Rashford as her 'real-life hero'

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 4, 2024

The Manchester United football star is dominating headlines

    Famed for her racy novels that are brimming with brooding heroes, Jilly Cooper's real-life hero happens to be a very special Premier League footballer - Marcus Rashford!

    During an exclusive interview in this week's , the 83-year-old author revealed: "It's Marcus Rashford. I think he's absolutely wonderful. Gosh, he's a good boy."

    The Manchester United player, who has been campaigning for free school meals for children during the holidays, appeals to Jilly's compassion and sense of fair play. "When you can't feed your children, it must be awful," she added. "Living in lockdown and being short of money is terrible."
