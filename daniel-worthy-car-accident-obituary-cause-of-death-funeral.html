Daniel Worthy Car Accident: Obituary, Cause Of Death, Funeral

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Unexpectedly, an admired student-athlete who graduated from Merrimack College at 22 has passed away. On June 21, 2023, the well-known Waynesboro, Pennsylvania, resident died in a dreadful car accident.

Daniel’s mother, who made the devastating announcement, requested prayers for the entire family as they struggled to cope with the catastrophe.

“Prayers would be appreciated for our family. Our hearts ache as we miss you so much while you are being carried in the arms of Jesus, but we have only good memories of our lovely, talented, funny-witted, and devoted son or brother.

About Daniel Worthy:

Years ago, when Daniel graduated from Merrimack College, he announced it on his blog: “After a successful four years at Merrimack College, I am excited and more than prepared for the next chapter in my playing career.” According to Daniel, he majored in Criminology.

He was a genuine young man everyone in Waynesboro admired and had an incredible enthusiasm for football.

Daniel Worthy was well-known at the school for his defensive lineman football career as player #98. He was a Merrimack Warriors player. Besides Northwest High School, he attended Merrimack before continuing his education there.

He devoted many years to serving his fellows in various capacities that allowed him to speak up for the needs and issues of his surroundings.

Condolences:

“I wasn’t prepared for this. I’m devastated. I don’t have the words to describe how much Daniel meant to me. He was such a light in this world. We were talking a little bit ago about how we would do so much this summer. My heart goes out to his family and the friends that he made his family. He made me feel like I was important. He always was so encouraging and always helped everyone in his path. I’m going to miss you, Daniel Worthy. Everyone is. Today is a sad, cruel day.”
