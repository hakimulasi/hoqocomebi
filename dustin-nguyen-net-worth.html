What is Dustin Nguyen's Net Worth?

Dustin Nguyen is an American actor, director, writer, and martial artist who has a net worth of $3 million. Dustin Nguyen was born in Saigon, South Vietnam in September 1962. He left Vietnam with his family in 1975 and he moved to the United States. As an actor he starred as Officer Harry Truman Ioki on the television series 21 Jump Street from 1987 to 1990.

Dustin Nguyen starred as Marc Wiessner on the TV series Die Gang in 1997. From 1999 to 2002 he starred as Johnny Loh on the series V.I.P. He has also had recurring roles on the TV series Magnum, P.I., SeaQuest 2032, and Phantom 2040. Dustin Nguyen has also starred in several films including Sunset Strip, Earth Angel, 3 Ninjas Kick Back, Vanishing Son II, Vanishing Son IV, Virtuosity, Finishing the Game: The Search for a New Bruce Lee, Saigon Eclipse, The Rebel, Open Upon a Time in Vietnam, 22 Jump Street, The Man with the Iron Fists 2, Jackpot, Zero Tolerance, and more.
