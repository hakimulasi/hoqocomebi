Terrance Perry Obituary, Bridgeville, Delaware, Victim Of Fatal Crash In Milton Identified By State

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 2, 2024

Terrance Perry Obituary, Death – Terrance Perry, 47 years old, a resident of Bridgeville, Delaware, was identified as the person who perished in the tragic accident that took place in Milton. This information was announced by the Delaware State Police. The Delaware State Police Troop 7 Collision Reconstruction Unit is continuing their investigation into the scene of the incident that resulted in the death of Terrance Perry.

For the purpose of assisting in the investigation, the authorities are actively seeking information and are urging any witnesses to come forward. The investigation is being led by Senior Corporal J. Smith, who is soliciting the assistance of anyone who may have witnessed the collision or who may have information that is pertinent to the investigation. Senior Corporal J. Smith can be reached at (302) 703-3267 if you are a witness or another individual who may have information.

You can also contact Delaware Crime Stoppers at 1 (800) 847-3333 or send a private message to the Facebook page of the Delaware State Police in order to share information in a manner that cannot be seen by others. This request for information highlights the ongoing efforts that law enforcement is making to gather details that are essential to comprehending the circumstances surrounding the fatal crash.

The Delaware State Police are looking for the community’s assistance in order to shed light on the incident and bring the investigation to a successful conclusion. The authorities are continuing their diligent efforts to unravel the details of the crash, and the identification of Terrance Perry is a significant step in the investigation process. The goal of the investigation is to provide answers and understanding for the grieving family and community that has been impacted by this tragic loss.
