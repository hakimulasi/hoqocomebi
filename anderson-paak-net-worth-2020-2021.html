Early Life & Biography

Brandon Paak Anderson was born on February 8, 1986, in Oxnard, California. His mother is of Korean and African-American descent who was born in Korea but was later adopted by an American family. Paak completed his schooling from Foothill Technology High School.

When he was seven years old, his father attacked his mother when Paak and his sister went out. His father was arrested and was jailed for 14 years.

Personal Life

Paak married his wife Jaylyn in the year 2011. Together they are the parents of two beautiful children Soul Rasheed, Shine. He lives happily with his wife and children in California and is able to keep his life away from the limelight.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 8 February 1986, Anderson Paak is 38 years old as of today’s date 20th July 2024. His height is 1.76 m tall, and his weight is 71 kg.

Career

Anderson Paak started his career producing music as a teenager. Initially, he used to perform as a drummer at a local church. Prior to his career as a musician, he worked at a marijuana farm in Santa Barbara.

In 2011, he was hired by Shafiq Husayn of the musical group Sa-Ra as an assistant, videographer, editor, writer, and producer. The following year, he released his debut album O.B.E. Vol.1. He also played as a drummer for the former American Idol semi-finalist Haley Reinhart.

In 2013, he remade popular songs of the 1950s, including soul, funk, jazz, left-field pop, hip-hop in an all-covers EP, Cover Art. The album was released by Hellfyre Club and OBE labels.

Paak also featured in the Watsky’s 2014 album All You Can Do. In 2014, he released his first album using his original name Anderson Paak. Previously he used different pseudonyms such as Breezy Lovejoy, Cheeky Andy, Almond Titfudge. In the same year, he joined the ‘Enter The Void’ tour of Jhené Aiko.

In 2016, Paak released his second music album Malibu which featured 9th Wonder, Rapsody, and Kaytranada. Later, he signed a contract with the record label Dr. Dre’s Aftermath Entertainment. He was named one of the XXL Freshmen, which include other artists such as Desiigner, G Herbo, 21 Savage, Lil Dicky, and others.

In 2018, he collaborated with Apple Inc and recorded the single “Til It’s Over” for their TV commercial of the Apple HomePod smart speaker. The single was also used in the 2018 video game Forza Horizon 4. In November of the same year, he released his third album Oxnard. The album featured many musicians, including Kendrick Lamar, Snoop Dogg, Pusha T, and J. Cole.

Paak was the songwriter for two songs of Christina Aguilera’s 2018 album, Liberation. In April 2019, he released his fourth studio album Ventura which featured artists including André 3000, Smokey Robinson, Brandy, and others. Along with his music group, The Free Nationals, he went on the tour “The Best Teef In the Game Tour ”.

Paak has also seen in the television game show The Price Is Right on January 2020. Recently, he featured on Eminem’s eleventh studio album titled “Music to be Murdered By.”

Awards & Achievements

Anderson Paak has won several awards and nominations in his illustrious career. He has won the prestigious Grammy Awards three times for his songs Bubblin, Ventura, and Come Home. In 2016, he also won the Soul Train Music Awards in the category of Centric Certified Award and Sweden GAFFA Awards in the category of Best Foreign New Act.

Paak has also been nominated for several awards, such as BET Hip Hop Awards (2016), Denmark GAFFA Awards (2019), Grammy Awards (2017), and Soul Train Music Awards (2016).

Net Worth & Salary of Anderson Paak in 2024

As of July 2024, Anderson Paak has an estimated net worth of more than $5 million. He has been able to make this huge fortune through his successful career as a singer, rapper, songwriter, record producer, and multi-instrumentalist.

He also performs at various musical tours, concerts, stage, and award shows, which also helps in increasing his net worth significantly.
