Frank Kozik Labbit Kidrobot Artist Cause Of Death

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

We learned that Frank Kozik has died. Frank who is a true giant of the scene- art, cars, toys, sculptures, underground art, music, rock posters, album covers, record labels passed away on Saturday, May 6, 2023, at the age of 61.

Frank Kozik was an acclaimed artist and lover of music, cats, and former man behind Man’s Ruin Records. He was always super cool person, he helped give the 90s it’s own graphic identity, along with a handful of other artists.

From his work creating iconic gig posters to his delightfully irreverent world-building with Labbits, Frank’s work is some of the earliest and most-beloved in the history of scene- arts.

This is a deverstating blow to those who knew and were close to him, he will be missed so dearly, please keep his family in yor thoughts and prayers.
