Where did Darren Waller go to high school?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 19, 2024

Darren Charles Waller is an American football tight end for the Las Vegas Raiders of the National Football League (NFL).

Where did Darren Waller go to high school?

Darren Waller was born on September 13, 1992, to Dorian and Charlena Waller in Landover, Maryland. His great-grandfather is renowned jazz musician Fats Waller.

Fats was a jazz pianist, organist, composer, violinist, singer, and comedic entertainer whose innovations in the Harlem stride style laid much of the basis for modern jazz piano.

Waller moved around a lot during his childhood. He was raised in Colorado Springs, Colorado, Marietta, Georgia, and Acworth, Georgia. He then attended the North Cobb High School in Kennesaw, Georgia.

After high school, he committed to playing college football at the Georgia Institute of Technology for the Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets.

There, he redshirted his freshman season in 2011. He made the decision to forgo his final year of eligibility to enter the 2015 NFL Draft.
