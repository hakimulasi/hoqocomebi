Mary Steenburgen Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Mary Steenburgen is a wealthy American actress with an estimated net worth of $80 million.

That is her combined net worth with her long-term husband, fellow actor Ted Danson.

Ted and Mary own a number of properties across the country, the majority of which are in the Los Angeles area. They sold a nine-acre property in Ojai, California, for $8.75 million in June 2019.

Ted and Mary paid $4.5 million for the house through a trust in 2005. They also own a home in Ojai that they purchased in 2017 for $2.4 million.

They paid $3.5 million for a home in Santa Monica, California in 2014, and then paid $5.2 million for the property next door in January 2019 to create a large private compound.

They also have a small cottage in Nashville and a six-acre estate on Martha’s Vineyard.

Throughout her career, Mary Steenburgen has appeared in a number of films. After winning an Oscar for Best Supporting Actress for her third film, “Melvin and Howard,” in 1980, she went on to appear in films such as “Ragtime,” “Cross Creek,” “Parenthood,” “Elf,” “The Help,” and “Book Club.”

Mary Steenburgen has also appeared on numerous television shows such as “Ink,” “Curb Your Enthusiasm,” “Joan of Arcadia,” “Justified,” and “Zoey’s Extraordinary Playlist.”
