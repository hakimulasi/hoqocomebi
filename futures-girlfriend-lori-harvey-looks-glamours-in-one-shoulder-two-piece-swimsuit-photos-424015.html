Futures Girlfriend, Lori Harvey, Looks Glamours In One-Shoulder Two-Piece Swimsuit Photos

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Lori Harvey has decided to break the Internet with a series of photos where she is wearing a stunning one-shoulder bikini.

Future’s girlfriend made jaws drop by flaunting her flawless figure in the two-piece swimsuit in an Instagram post.

Steve Harvey’s stepdaughter completed the look with several pieces of jewelry and a sleek hairdo. Many followers praised Lori for her glamorous shoot.

She did do an interview where she said this about her privacy: “It’s definitely frustrating. I think I’ve developed tough skin through it all. If someone wants to think the worst of you, they’re going to think the worst of you no matter what you say. You can’t defend a lie. I think it’s a lot of false information about me in the media because clickbait is very real. I think that’s one thing the public doesn’t understand. I don’t think there’s an article you can find about me that doesn’t have “allegedly” or “rumored” in it. Because there’s no confirmation, and they don’t care to fact check.”

https://www.instagram.com/p/CDKhV14FUn4/

She added: “So that’s the frustrating part because you’re like actually dealing with someone’s real life. But they don’t care they want the clicks and people will believe anything. But my family knows what’s going on. My friends know what’s going on.”

Lori revealed the following about her famous Jamaica trip: “That was my first time in Jamaica, and I had the time of my life. I have a newfound love for Jamaica now. We had too much fun. I think the last day, which was my birthday, I said, ‘I want to have so much fun that I don’t know if you guys are having fun.’ That’s how much fun I want to have. And I overachieved that goal. Everybody was throwing up at the end of the night. Everybody was dead the next morning. Everybody trying to get on the plane was like, ‘Did you throw up? Yeah, did you throw up? Yeah.’ I have the best friends ever because they all cleared their schedules to come. I didn’t expect everyone to make it, but they all made it.”
