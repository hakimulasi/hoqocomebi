Early Life & Biography

Gwendoline Christie was born in Worthing, Sussex, on October 28, 1978. She was born into a middle-class family. Her father used to work in sales and marketing while her mother was a housewife. She also has two half-brothers. Both of them are older than her. When she was a child, she used to train as a gymnast, but due to a spinal injury, she had to give it up. She then took up acting. She studied at Henfield Primary School and later graduated from Warden Park Secondary School. After finishing school, she went to the Drama Centre, London. She graduated from there in 2005.

Career

Her character soon gained popularity, and she became a fan favorite. In addition to her role as Brienne of Tarth, she has also appeared in the science-fantasy series Wizards and Aliens from 2012 to 2013.

She then played Commander Lyne in the 2015 film, The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 2. She then co-starred in Star Wars: The Force Awakens as one of the antagonists, Captain Phasma. She again appeared in Star Wars: The Last Jedi playing the same role.

In 2018, she played Lady Jane in The Darkest Minds and as Gwen in the film In Fabric.
