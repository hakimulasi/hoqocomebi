Who Is Elvis Figueroa? Everything To Know About The Professional Boxer

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Boxer Elvis Figueroa, aged 25, is set to face Ryan Adams on 7 January 2022. Learn everything about his height, ethnicity, and personal life via this article.

Elvis Figueroa is a professional American boxer who fights out of New Haven, Connecticut. He is currently on the headlines for his upcoming match with Ryan Adams.

Figueroa trained hard and remained an amateur until 2017. Until now, he plays in the super middle division in the boxing championships.

Elvis debuted professionally from a match against Devison Riberio on 28 October 2017. Since then, he has not lost a single match and today's match will be a deciding factor.

Thus, fans are eager to find out the result of the ongoing match between Figueroa and Ryan.
