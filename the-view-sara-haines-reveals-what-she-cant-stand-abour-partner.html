The View star Sara Haines reveals what she 'can't stand' about partner

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 19, 2024

The conversation took place on Thursday morning, with the other panelists laughing at Sara's confession – but do you agree, or does your partner do this too? Find out what she had to say here…
