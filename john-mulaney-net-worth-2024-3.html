Early Life and Career Beginnings

John Mulaney was born in Chicago, Illinois, and grew up in a family with an interest in the arts. He attended Georgetown University, where he studied English and minored in Theology. After college, Mulaney moved to New York City to pursue a career in comedy. His early work included a stint at “Saturday Night Live” where he contributed significantly as a writer and occasionally appeared on the show’s “Weekend Update” segment.

Rise to Fame
