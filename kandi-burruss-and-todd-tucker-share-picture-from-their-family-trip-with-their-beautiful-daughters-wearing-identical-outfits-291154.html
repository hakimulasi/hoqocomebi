Kandi Burruss And Todd Tucker Share Picture From Their Family Trip With Their Beautiful Daughters We

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 8, 2024

Corny but very adorable! Kandi Burruss has taken to social media, where she delighted fans with the sweetest picture of her entire family.

The sweet family portrait features, Kandi, her husband, Todd Tucker, and almost all of their children — Kaela and Riley — in matching outfits.

The Tucker/Burruss clan took the photo at an Atlanta airport, and they all wore the same cozy T-shirt that reads “This is my airport T-shirt.”

Little Ace did not have a shirt, so many fans bombarded Kandi and Todd on the matter. Mama Joyce also failed to wear a matching outfit with the rest of the family.

Shout out to all of the positive people! Just adopt me that’s all I’m asking I cook and clean 😂One day I want to come off vacation so I can go back on vacation. Y’all have fun!!!”

Kandi and Todd’s family trip comes just days after a lavish vacation with Riley.

The Xscape singer and her teen daughter flew to Tokyo, where they experienced all that is exotic and fun in the Asian country.

Kandi shared multiple photos from Japan and explained: “If you go to #Tokyo you HAVE to do the #streetkarttokyobaybbq experience! @rileyburruss had soooo much fun! You ride around the entire city in go karts dressed like cartoon characters. YOU HAVE TO HAVE AN INTERNATIONAL DRIVERS PERMIT TO DO THIS. It’s easy to get. Go online get the application & send in with 2 passport photos. I got mine only a week before my trip. You have to be 18 tho. Unfortunately, Riley couldn’t drive one, but she still had fun riding with our guide. #mommydaughtertrip #kanditrip”
