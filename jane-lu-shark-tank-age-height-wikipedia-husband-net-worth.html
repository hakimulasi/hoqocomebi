Who Is Jane Lu? Her Wiki, Biography, Background 

Jane Lu was born on 12 June 1986 to Chinese-Australian parents Queenie, 61, and Frank Lu, 67. Jane Lu is a businesswoman famous for founding Showpo, a fashion boutique. She started the business in 2010 after leaving Ernst & Young where she worked as an analyst.

Talking about her career, the business initially started as a pop-up shop with a friend, but after it failed and left Lu in debt, she started her own business. The business, now called Showpo, proved to be a success and has increased in value each year.

Lu was named Cosmopolitan Entrepreneur of the Year in 2015, and in 2016, she was listed in the Forbes Asia “30 under 30” list and in Smart Company’s “Hot 30 under 30” list in both 2016 and 2017.
