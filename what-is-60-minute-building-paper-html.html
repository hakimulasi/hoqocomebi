What is 60 minute building paper?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Answer

60-Minute, single ply building paper is a weather-resistant barrier that is designed to provide excellent secondary protection behind exterior cladding in order to prevent moisture penetration and condensation in exterior wall assemblies. 60-Minute, single ply building paper is available in a variety of colours and sizes. This product is an asphalt-saturated, craft grade D, breather-type sheathing building paper with a breather-type sheathing construction.

In the same way, what is the purpose of construction paper?

For starters, the objective of building paper is to prevent wind from getting into a wall, and then it serves as a secondary moisture barrier behind the cladding. In rainy climates, the second function is very significant since moisture in a wall is not something that should be tolerated.

A grade D, heavy kraft paper that has been wet with asphalt, the Hal-Tex 30 Minute Heavy Breathable Building Paper provides 30 minutes of breathability. What exactly is a WRB?

Generally speaking, a water-resistant barrier (WRB) refers to a substance that is put between the sheathing (or studs if there is no sheathing) and the siding of a structure. In order to prevent water from reaching building components that may be harmed by moisture, it is installed on the roof.
