Zendaya Edition: Top 23 Show-Stopping Red Carpet Moments

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Picture this: a runway wrapped in red, buzzing with anticipation as a figure steps out, ready to own the night. That’s Zendaya for you – a maven of the red carpet and the quintessential triple-threat: actress, singer, and fashion sorceress who spins every event into a mesmerizing fashion spectacle.

Her style game is so sharp it could cut diamonds; she has a chameleon-like quality that makes the concept of a signature look seem positively boring. Thanks to her partnership with the stylist and ‘image architect’ Law Roach, who’s a masterclass in sartorial innovation and transformed her into one of the most anticipated and revered figures at every red-carpet event. From captivating hot pink breastplates to jaw-dropping Balmain leather dresses, each appearance is a meticulously crafted spectacle that defines her evolving style journey, leaving a trail of unforgettable moments etched in the history of fashion!

As the curtains draw on 2023, we are dissecting 23 fashion moments when Zendaya ruled the red carpet with panache, a celebration of her style journey and magnetic allure. Scroll through to take a trip down memory lane!

Be A Desi Girl!

Zendaya and her beau Tom Holland graced the star-studded NMACC event scene in Mumbai in style. She glided down the red carpet alongside her stylist extraordinaire, Law Roach, donning a stunning custom Rahul Mishra Couture creation. This ensemble, marrying a hand-embroidered saree gown with a dazzling ‘Flying Cranes’ golden bralette, was impeccably accessorized with Bulgari Serpenti Jewelry, coupled with a refreshingly minimal makeup look. It was a fusion of tradition and trend, exuding elegance at its finest.

Vintage Versace

The reigning queen of style graced the 54th NAACP Image Awards with an entrance that could only be described as jaw-dropping. Dressed to the nines in a vintage Versace ensemble from the coveted 2002 collection, she left the crowd awestruck. Her choice—a stunning strapless black gown, intricately designed with a cut-away bodice, bold hips, and splashes of vibrant green that screamed sophistication. But that wasn’t all. Zendaya’s glamour game was on point, with a chic chocolate-brown bob cascading in soft waves, reminiscent of ’90s elegance. Her makeup? A nod to the era, too—picture a bronzed smoky eye paired with a heavily lined nude matte lip that amplified her natural beauty to new heights. She didn’t just walk the red carpet; she owned it with her impeccable style and effortless grace.

Starry Delight

At the same event, Zendaya chose a revamped Prada SS1993 outfit as her second ensemble that redefined elegance. She transformed the original runway style, swapping trousers for a stunning floor-length white skirt with a chic waist cut-out. It was elevated with a celestial duchesse bralette and matching white pointed pumps, complemented by dazzling Bulgari jewellery. Zendaya’s fashion finesse was on full display, turning heads and setting a new standard for sophisticated glam.

Rose Bouquet, Anyone?

Zendaya stole the spotlight at the 2023 SAG Awards, leaving jaws dropped in a breathtaking Valentino masterpiece. Picture this: a bespoke powder-pink silk gown crafted by none other than the genius Pierpaolo Piccioli, adorned with a show-stopping train blooming with 190 silk roses. Designed to perfection by Piccioli and expertly styled by Law Roach, Zendaya looked like the epitome of pure elegance and glamour.

Next Bond Girl

Zendaya stole the show at the Louis Vuitton SS24 event during Paris Fashion Week, exuding Bond girl elegance in a scuba-style figure-hugging white dress adorned with gold and leather buckle straps. Her daring ensemble, adorning a plunging neckline and thigh-high slit, embodied sophistication and glamour, captivating all with her stunning fashion statement.

Sizzle in Red

Zendaya wowed her fans with a series of breathtaking photos, showcasing her in a striking viscose boucle dress from Bottega Veneta’s winter 2023 collection, radiating Judy Jetson vibes with its exaggerated neckline and flared skirt in a bold red hue, the trending colour of the season. Even her beau, Tom Holland, was left speechless, leaving fire emojis “???” in the comments. It’s safe to say the pictures speak volumes on their own!

Jaw-dropped? Us too!

At the Valentino SS23 show during Paris Fashion Week in October 2022, Zendaya stunned in a braless, totally sheer catsuit layered under leather hot pants and an oversized glittery blazer embossed with the fashion house’s logo. Her soft curls, complemented by a side-part hairstyle, and a subtle yet glamorous makeup look, teamed with a soft smoky eye and a moody autumnal lip, enhanced her appearance as a quintessential Valentino muse.

Spiderwoman

Zendaya wowed at the LA premiere of Spider-Man: No Way Home, dazzling in a mesmerizing custom Maison Valentino slip dress. The nude gown, intricately designed with a deep-V neckline, a low-scooped back, and elegant black spiderweb detailing, paid homage to her relationship with beau and co-star Tom Holland. This stunning appearance echoes her prior spidey-inspired fashion moment in London, where she donned another exquisite Valentino gown adorned with elegant black spiderwebs.

Old Hollywood Glam

Zendaya stunned at a Bulgari event in Venice, donning a custom black velvet corset dress by Richard Quinn, reminiscent of the iconic mid-century styles of Elizabeth Taylor and Dorothy Dandridge. The dress featured a plunging neckline and a chic fishtail hem, paying homage to the glamour of the Old Hollywood era.

Bold Tigress

Zendaya made a striking entrance at the 2023 Louis Vuitton show at PFW, sporting an audacious triple tiger print ensemble that set a new standard for boldness and fierceness. The coordinated outfit, paired with knee-high boots and a chic top-handle bag, exuded an epic vibe that stole the spotlight. Complementing her look was a glamorous old Hollywood-style bob, adding a touch of timeless elegance to the daring ensemble.

Tropical Queen

Zendaya, at the 2017 Met Gala, was a showstopper in a stunning Dolce & Gabbana Alta Moda gown. She boldly defied convention with a vibrant Pappagalli carousel print, accentuating her beauty with daring choices like a striking red lip. Despite differing from the theme, her tropical ballgown with parrot prints showcased her refusal to conform, cementing her as a confident trendsetter that night.

Desert Warrior

Zendaya debuted at the 2021 Venice Film Festival in a stunning custom leather Balmain dress, reminiscent of her ‘Dune’ character, Chani. The desert-warrior-inspired attire effortlessly merged boldness with elegance. She elevated the ensemble with a Bulgari necklace, graced with opulent emeralds, adding a touch of glamour to her look.

Joan of Arc

Zendaya made a dramatic entrance at the 2018 Met Gala, channelling Joan of Arc in a bold Versace iconic gown and a striking red hairstyle that perfectly captured the theme. Sporting a blunt auburn bob with micro bangs, Zendaya donned a full-on armour dress by Versace, a daring choice that not only paid homage to the event’s theme, “Heavenly Bodies: Fashion and the Catholic Imagination,” but also showcased her impeccable style. Few could pull off such a powerful ensemble, but Zendaya effortlessly merged vampy Versace sequins with towering platforms, creating a saintly interpretation of Gala’s divine fashion theme.

Emerald Elegance

At the 2019 Emmys, Zendaya lit up the red carpet in a breathtaking emerald green Vera Wang gown. The custom-made dress beautifully complemented her glowing complexion, reminiscent of a modern-day Poison Ivy, leaving us eagerly anticipating more dazzling partnerships between Zendaya and renowned design studios.

Tangerine Delight

Zendaya’s fashion finesse continues to stun, and with this outfit, she ate and left no crumbs! Her latest jaw-dropping outfit—a Ferragamo tangerine two-piece—appears as a transparent orange ensemble at first glance. However, a closer look will reveal its intricate layers, featuring a vibrant citrus-hued fabric beneath a sheer burnt orange overlay. This blend of boldness and functionality defines Zendaya’s captivating style choices, making her stand out!

Bedazzling Beauty

In a jaw-dropping fashion spectacle at the 2022 Oscars, Zendaya emerged as the epitome of sophistication in a Valentino haute couture masterpiece. Picture this: a daring micro, ab-baring pearl white silk button-up flawlessly harmonizing with a shimmering silver sequined skirt. Her ensemble echoed the audacious spirit of Sharon Stone’s groundbreaking 1998 red-carpet ensemble, creating an instant fashion time warp. Zendaya’s choice served as a bold testament to her evolving style. This look didn’t just turn heads; it demanded attention, cementing her position as a trendsetter in the ever-evolving world of haute couture.

Certified Fashion Icon

Zendaya strutted onto the CFDA Fashion Awards stage in New York and snagged the coveted 2021 Fashion Icon Award—no surprises there! She pulled out all the stops, flaunting a sizzling two-piece scarlet Vera Wang ensemble. Picture this: a daringly small bralette paired with a peplum-style skirt that cascaded from her hips to the ground. Talk about jaw-dropping! To accessorize, she rocked a sleek round silver necklace, and a Bulgari bracelet, and stole the show with stunning thigh-length braids that tied the whole look together.

Dune Premiere

Zendaya’s romp through the ‘Dune’ press tour is fashion’s version of a red carpet revolution! Leicester Square lights up as she strides forth, a vision in an asymmetrical white sequin masterpiece by Rick Owens. This gown isn’t just a showstopper; it’s a jaw-dropper, worth its weight in fashion gold. But wait, there’s more! Zendaya sprinkles her magic with silver rings, bracelets, and stud earrings, sealing the deal on a look that screams perfection. Seriously, this ensemble has me swooning!

A Vision in Pink

At the 2020 Critics’ Choice Awards, Zendaya orchestrated a fashion symphony that left jaws dropped and screens shattered. Draped in a sizzling hot pink Tom Ford gown, she didn’t just steal the spotlight, she owned it. What set hearts racing? Zendaya fearlessly rocked Tom Ford’s boldest creation of the spring/summer 2020 season—a mesmerizingly audacious breastplate that whispered “sexy-tough” to the fashion gods. This wasn’t just a dress; it was a proclamation—a statement of style that rewrote the rules of Hollywood glamour. Witnessing Zendaya navigate the red carpet in this ensemble was like watching a masterclass in elegance. Heads turned, eyes widened, and the internet? Well, it kind of imploded.

Hello, Sunshine!

In a swoop of Hollywood grandeur, Zendaya graced the 2021 Oscars in a radiant, front-cut yellow Valentino gown, epitomizing a modern-day fairy. Designed by Valentino Haute Couture, this dress didn’t just turn heads—it caused the internet to spiral into a frenzy, and for good reason! But here’s the kicker: this gown was more than just a fashion statement; it was glow-in-the-dark magic. A true showstopper, Zendaya’s Valentino haute couture creation undeniably owned the spotlight, sealing her place in the annals of Oscar fashion perfection.

Modern Day Cinderella

Zendaya turned heads as a real-life Cinderella at the 2019 Met Gala, lighting up the red carpet in a custom Tommy Hilfiger gown. With her stylist Law Roach playing the part of her fairy godmother, complete with a wand illuminating her dress, the duo took Gala’s theme to another level of enchantment. Decked out in a gown that sparkled like a chic Christmas tree with a few magical waves, Zendaya’s look, accompanied by a carriage-shaped bag, was a dazzling blend of style and spectacle perfectly fitting the evening’s “Camp: Notes on Fashion” theme.

New-age Barbie

Zendaya’s Spider-Man: Homecoming promotional tour was an absolute style carnival! Among her stunning choices, one particular gown deserves a standing ovation. Picture this: a jaw-dropping, pink Ralph & Russo masterpiece, channelling Barbie vibes with a train that seemed to go on forever. Paired flawlessly with complementing pink pumps and luscious chocolate brown curls, Zendaya redefined her red carpet game, shattering the norms of premiere dressing and keeping tongues wagging in the fashion world for weeks.

A Shimmering Beauty

At the swanky Vanity Fair Oscars Party of 2020, Zendaya stole the show in a dazzling nude Bulgari gown, complete with a sophisticated hair bun that screamed elegance. Her red carpet prowess effortlessly flowed into the after-party scene, cementing her reign as a style icon from start to finish.

Zendaya’s journey through the red carpet has been a vibrant tapestry of style, innovation, and unbridled charisma. Each appearance is a testament to her evolution as a fashion luminary, challenging norms and setting new benchmarks. As we revel in her trailblazing fashion story, we await the next chapter, knowing Zendaya will undoubtedly continue to enchant, surprise, and reign supreme on the red carpet stage.
