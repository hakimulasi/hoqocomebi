All you need to know about AJ Odudu's love life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 29, 2024

The Celebrity Big Brother presenter has been coy about her love life

    AJ Odudu is back to present Celebrity Big Brother! A regular fixture on our screens, the 36-year-old has gone from strength to strength in her career, hosting and the flagship series Big Brother in recent years. 

    Adding to her ever-expanding portfolio, in 2021 AJ also appeared on Strictly Come Dancing, where she came in third and sparked romance rumours with her dance partner, Kai Widdrington. 

    An extremely private person, AJ rarely speaks about her life outside of work. But, is the talented TV star single? And what has she said in the past? takes a closer look…

    AJ is currently believed to be single. The star prefers to keep her love life private although she has opened up about her dating history. 

    During an episode of AJ revealed that dating hasn't always been easy. "I’ve been cheated on by three ex-boyfriends," she said. "One of them was sleeping with my best friends." The presenter has never named any of her former boyfriends.

    During her time on series 19 of , fans were once convinced that AJ and her dance partner, Kai Widdrington, were dating. 

    While appearing on BBC Radio 2, DJ Zoe Ball asked if she and Kai had become more than friends after their passionate routine for movie week, which was based on the hit film, "Some of the newspapers are trying to allude – and I think everyone is hoping and praying – that maybe, romance…?" Zoe remarked to the star. 

    As Zoe then noted that AJ was giggling, the star insisted: "I'm just giggling! Guys, I was in character at that moment on the dance floor this week."

    She continued: "I was playing Rachel, he was playing Frank [from The Bodyguard]. They were in love!" Zoe then joked: "Bit of roleplay there, that's how it all begins," prompting further laughter from AJ. 

    After shutting down romance reports, AJ went on to speak about how difficult the media attention had been for herself and Kai. Chatting to Mail Online in 2022, she admitted: "It is hard when you're receiving a lot of attention and are constantly asked the same questions but honestly Kai and myself were so focused and we just wanted it to be the best experience it could be."

    She added: "It was a dream show for Kai, it was a dream show for me, and I will always be Kai's first ever dance partner on Strictly and I had the most incredible experience with him and formulated a strong bond with him and the rest of the people on the show last year. The cast formulated such genuine bonds outside of the show."

    As of January 2022, Kai is dating fellow Strictly pro, Nadiya Bychkova.
