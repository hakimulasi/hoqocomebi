Lisa Rinna Gets Extensions in Response to Brandi Glanville's Hair Diss

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Long hair ’cause she just don’t care about disses!

Making sure to have the last laugh, Lisa Rinna fired back at her Real Housewives of Beverly Hills costar Brandi Glanville (who complained that Rinna’s had the same hairstyle for 20 years) by hitting the Access Hollywood stage on Wednesday, April 8, with long, comically lush extensions.

“Say hello to the new Lisa Rinna everybody!” Access host Billy Bush announced when Rinna, 51, entered the studio. He continued, “You’re the top trending topic on Yahoo!, by the way, and your hair is probably half the reason.”

While Bush told the Melrose Place alum that she looked “great,” Rinna had qualms. “I feel like a Kardashian!” she laughed.

But then, Bush noted that the added locks made the actress and reality TV star look like she was about 30 years old, which perked Rinna up instantly. “Well, God, then I need to grow my hair!” she said. “Okay, maybe Brandi was right. I’ll give it to her. If I’m going to look 30, I’m going to go there.”

The Housewives’ argument first began when Rinna teased Glanville during the RHOBH reunion episode. The actress said that a lowbrow character she once played on CSI: Crime Scene Investigation was inspired by Glanville, to which the former model replied, “You’ve had the same hairdo for 20 years, and you’re basing it on my look?”
