Mega Millions, May 24th, 2022: The numbers for the $143 million jackpot

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

The Mega Millions lottery selected the winning numbers in its Tuesday, May 24, 2022 drawing for an estimated $143 million jackpot.

The numbers are 3 - 5 - 6 - 63 - 68 Mega Ball 24 Megaplier 3x.

The mega jackpot was 3x

The jackpot was an estimated $143 million with a cash option of $83.6 million.

Meanwhile, the Powerball jackpot is at $135 million, with a cash option of $79.2 million, according to the Powerball website.

Until recently, lottery winners in New Jersey had to be identified, but now winners will be able to remain anonymous under a new law that was signed by Governor Phil Murphy.

How to play Mega Millions?

  • Mega Millions tickets cost $2.00 per play
  • Players may choose six numbers from two separate pools of numbers - five different numbers from 1 to 70 (the white balls) and one number from 1 to 25 (the gold Mega Ball) - or select Easy Pick/Quick Pick. You win the jackpot by matching all six winning numbers in a drawing.
  • Until further notice, the starting jackpot will vary based on sales and will be announced before each drawing.
  • There are a total of nine ways to win a prize in Mega Millions, ranging from the jackpot down to $2
